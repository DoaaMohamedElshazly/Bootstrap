What are Networks & Communications?

Networks and communications form the backbone of modern technology. A network is a system of interconnected devices, allowing seamless data transfer. It enables computers, mobile devices, and servers to communicate efficiently through various protocols and technologies.

Key Components of Networking:

  • Wired Networks: Ethernet cables, fiber optics, and traditional LAN connections.
  • Wireless Networks: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and mobile data (3G, 4G, 5G).
  • Cybersecurity: Protection against cyber threats, encryption techniques, and firewalls.
  • Cloud Computing: Internet-based data storage and access.
